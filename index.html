--- layout: archive author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} A little bit about me...
Broadly speaking, I pursue research in artificial intelligence. More specifically, my interest lies in applying deep learning techniques to solve computer vision, NLP and reinforcement learning problems.

It is fascinating to see that a composition of multiple non-linear transformations can yeild a more abstract – and ultimately more useful – representation. I derive a lot of my motivation from the universality of neural networks and questioning their true expressive powers.

My other hobbies include badminton, travelling and trekking. Formally, I was a member of the N.S.O Athletics team, IITR.
Studying in Roorkee gave me the unique benefit of camping in the Himalayas, white-water rafting in Rishikesh and paragliding in Bir Billing.
