--- layout: archive author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} A little bit about me...
I pursue my research in artificial intelligence. My interests lie in applying deep learning techniques to solve computer vision, NLP and reinforcement learning problems. I derive a lot of my motivation from the universality of neural networks and questioning their true expressive powers.

Having worked on diverse yet simple problems across various domains, I have gained a decent understanding of deep learning techniques, its priors and its contemporaries.


My other hobbies include badminton, travelling and trekking. Formally, I was a member of the N.S.O Athletics team, IITR.
Studying in Roorkee gave me the unique benefit of trekking in the Himalayas, white-water rafting in Rishikesh and Paragliding in Bir Billing, all over weekends and long vacations.
