--- layout: archive author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} I am a visiting researcher hosted by Prof. Asif Ekbal at AI-NLP-ML lab, IIT Patna. My research focuses broadly on probablistic machine learning including topics on sequence modeling, reinforcement learning, meta-learning, lifelong learning and generative modeling.

I find it fascinating that we, humans, have an intuitive understanding of serial arrangement, enabling us to continually learn from a temporal sequence of observations and actions. In contrast, for an artificial agent to be just as seamless, it would need to master several long-standing problems in artificial intelligence (AI). The objective of my research is to address these open problems, which makes me particularly excited about (i) reinforcement learning for sequential decision-making, (ii) sequence modeling and lifelong learning with recurrent neural networks.

My other hobbies include badminton, travelling and trekking. Formally, I was a member of the N.S.O Athletics team, IITR.
Studying in Roorkee gave me the unique benefit of camping in the Himalayas, white-water rafting in Rishikesh and paragliding in Bir Billing.
